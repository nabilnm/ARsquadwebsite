About us

OUR GOAL

"Take a step into the future"

That's really the basis of our project. We grew tired of receiving boring business cards from companies and solo startups during events and regular meets. Innovating a business card may sound irrelevant but you would be surprised to see the potential with augmented reality. A whole world of virtual objects are available at your disposal without the need of downloading any apps. We strive to create creative interactive business cards with use of virtual objects.

OUR MISSION

We create interactive business cards using augmented reality, the ability to bring forth any object our clients desire allows for intuitive environments. Interactive interfaces, clickable objects to perform specific actions, animated objects or even video playing interfaces scarcely scratch the surface of the possibilities to enrich your business card. all in all

"We create cool environments for boring business cards."
